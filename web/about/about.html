About us

At RHEMA, we believe that every Christian has a calling to serve in the Kingdom of God. RHEMA was founded in 1975 to train and equip Christians to fulfill the call of God upon their lives. RHEMA is present in 48 countries with over 40,000 graduates worldwide.

Birthed in 2001, RHEMA Singapore began with a mandate to impact all of Asia with the message of faith. We exist to impart the Word of God to our students in Singapore, and to teach them how to move in the Spirit with God's anointing.

As a student of RHEMA Singapore, you will discover what your gifts and calling are, how you can lead a successful Christian life, and how you can practically serve God. Our curriculum is designed to be spiritually transforming and practical in application, thereby equipping you for the purpose which the Lord has for you!

Vision

Our vision is to train men and women of all ages and vocations to be well-grounded in the Word of God, so that they can live successfully and reach their world for Jesus Christ.

Mission

Our mission is to empower people to reach people!
